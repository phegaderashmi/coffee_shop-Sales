Summary :

This project analyzes sales data to understand customer behavior, product performance, and overall business trends.

	Sales by Day & Hour
Sales are higher on weekdays, with peak activity during morning (8–11 AM) and evening (4–7 PM) hours.
	Peak Sales Time
The highest sales activity is observed during mid-morning and evening hours, especially on Fridays.

	Monthly Sales Trend
Sales show a steady increase across months, with May and June recording the highest revenue.

	Store Location Performance
Sales and footfall vary by location. Some stores consistently perform better, indicating location-based demand differences.

	Average Order Value
The average order value per customer remains consistent, showing stable customer spending behavior.

	Best-Selling Products
Barista Espresso and Hot Chocolate are the top-selling products in terms of both quantity and revenue.

	Sales by Category & Type
Beverages, especially coffee-based items, contribute the most to overall sales.
